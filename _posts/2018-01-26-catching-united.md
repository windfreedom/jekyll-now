If I was forced to use one word to define the the healthcare industry at end of 2017 it would be **merged**.


The sheer number and scale of M&A transactions in 2017 was staggering. We saw hospital large national hospital systems like Aurora  and Advocate Healthcare combine, industry giants like UnitedHealthGroup expand through the acquisition of DaVita Medical Group's outpatient dialysis clinics, and most famously,  CVS and Aetna decided to merge in a move that created perhaps the first large-scale combination of an insurer and pharmacy chain.
